Questions

What is Vedic Astrology, and how does it differ from Western astrology?

Vedic Astrology, or Jyotish, is an ancient Indian system based on the position of planets. It's more precise than Western astrology.

How can Vedic Astrology help in making life decisions?

It provides insights into various life aspects, helping with career, relationships, health, and personal growth.

What information do I need to provide for a Vedic Astrology consultation?

You need to provide your date, time, and place of birth for an accurate reading.

Can Vedic Astrology predict the future?

Vedic Astrology offers guidance and predictions based on planetary positions, helping you prepare for upcoming challenges and opportunities.
